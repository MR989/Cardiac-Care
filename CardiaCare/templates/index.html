Heart Disese Prediction System

Heart Disease is the leading cause of the death all over the world. Prediction of Heart Attack can be a very complicated process.​ In healthcare, especially in the field of cardiology, timely and efficient detection of heart disease plays a key role. Early prediction of heart disease can help in curtailing the causes of mortality. ​ By the help of machine learning algorithm we can predict the Heart Health.​

Coronary heart disease (CHD) is a condition in which the coronary arteries become clogged, resulting in symptoms such as angina, chest pain, and heart attacks.

Cardiac Arrest is one of the biggest causes of morbidity and mortality among the population of the world. ​

Heart disease is even highlighted as a silent killer which leads to death of a person without any obvious symptoms.

Hence prediction of cardiovascular disease is regarded as one of the most important subjects in the section of clinical data analysis.​
